About Vivian & Human Matching

Is Vivian really the one reading my profile?

Yes — every profile submitted goes through Vivian or her core matching team. We believe real people create real connections.

Is my data safe with Vivian?

Absolutely. Vivian's background in relationship mediation ensures your privacy is protected. Nothing is ever shared without your consent.

Can I talk to Vivian before signing up?

Of course! You can connect directly with her via WhatsApp after submitting your interest. She replies personally when possible.

Trust & Our Principles

Will my information be stored or shared?

No. None of your information is stored long-term or used for any advertising purposes. Everything is processed securely for one-time review by our human team.

Why must I provide honest, detailed information?

Because we care equally about protecting both men and women. Real information helps us ensure fairness, transparency, and emotional safety for all parties involved. We intentionally move slow and verify each case to prevent emotional harm.

Why is your process slower than other platforms?

Because we don’t rush love. Every profile is reviewed personally. There are no bots or instant matches—only real humans working for meaningful connections.
